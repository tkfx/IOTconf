Did you know that by 2020 there will be 30 billion connected devices?!

Internet of Things (IoT) is a world where 'things', both physical and vitual, are linked, and is set to transform daily life as we know it.

This exclusive, invitation-only event will bring together global thought leaders and business executives who are passionate about innovation and the advancement of technology.

Join us at this groundbreaking event.

Eran Wagner - Gemini Israel Ventures

Eran Wagner joined Gemini in 2011 as General Partner. His areas of investment focus include enterprise software, telecommunications and security; he also shows keen interest in the juncture of sensor networks, robotics and real-world applications. Eran brings to Gemini over two decades of rich entrepreneurial experience and executive knowhow. Prior to Gemini, Eran served as the President of North American Operations at cVidya Networks and Vice President, Portfolio Strategy and Planning at Amdocs, which he joined after a company he co-founded, XACCT Technologies Inc., was acquired by Amdocs in 2004. Eran holds a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science from Tel Aviv University.

Yossi Vardi - Angel Investor and Entrepreneur

Joseph “Yossi” Vardi is an angel investor and hi-tech entrepreneur and Chairman of International Technologies. He has some 40 years of experience of co-founding, leading and helping build over 70 high-tech companies and was the founding investor and the former chairman of Mirabilis Ltd, the creator of the highly popular instant messaging program ICQ, which was acquired by AOL, to which he still serves as an advisor. He is a former Director-General of the Ministries of Development and Energy, a former Chairman of Israel Chemicals, and the Israel National Oil Company as well as Chairman Emeritus of the Jerusalem Foundation. He is a member of the World Economic Forum and has served on the Advisory Board of the Bank of Israel, on the board of directors of State of Israel Bonds, on the Board of Governors of the Hebrew University and the Open University.

Volker Ziegler - Nokia Solutions & Networks

Volker Ziegler is the Chief Architect of Nokia Solutions and Networks. His main mission is to look far into the future and to lead the evolution of NSN offerings and related architecture for the upcoming 10 years and beyond. Volker has more than 20 years of Telco and IT industry experience and has an excellent understanding of both Technology and Business.  He holds a Ph.D in Electrical Engineering and his business experience includes being in charge of one of NSN key geographical regions.

Avi Baum - Texas Instruments

Avi Baum, networking and communication technologies veteran for the past 10 years. Serving today as the CTO of the connectivity group in Texas Instruments IL. In his role, Avi is bringing the IoT vision into connectivity products, helping shape them for the IoT era. In his former roles Avi was the chief architect for TI WiFi connectivity solutions, leading the architecture and product definition department.

Keith Saft - Telefonica Digital

Keith Saft is the Head BLNK.io at Telefonica Digital. After graduating from Carnegie Mellon University, Keith founded an interactive agency and taught interface design at CMU for several years before being recruited to Silicon Valley where he worked at Palm and then Yahoo—running yahoo.com globally and generating numerous patents. In 2010, Keith joined Telefonica Digital in Madrid and currently in London where he ran their M2M product development group and built their SIM Management platform which recently won a 20 million SIM contract by the UK government to manage their Smart Meter rollout. Late this Fall, Keith has launched a Telefonica funded internal startup—BLNK.io—around small data intelligence for the internet of things.

Itay Frishman - GKH Law

Itay Frishman is the head of the Venture Law group at GKH Law, and concentrates his practice in the area of corporate and securities law, with an emphasis on M&A, venture capital, financing, public and private offerings, complex cross-border executive compensation and tax matters. Itay serves on the Advisory Board of the Boston Israel Business Forum, a U.S. non-for-profit organization, dedicated to exploring business opportunities involving the United States and Israel. Itay joined GKH in 2004. Prior to that, Itay worked as a corporate associate at a law firm in Boston, Massachusetts. He holds an LLB from the Interdisciplinary Center in Herzliya, Israel.

Patrice Slupowski - Orange

Patrice Slupowski is Vice-President Digital Innovation & Communities running NExT.com, an innovation team transforming trends into products. The team is in charge of various innovative projects on mobile, PC, TV, tablets like TVcheck or Bodyguru and strongly involved in advanced social networks, content recommendation, social and interactive TV, personal data aggregation or e-education. Patrice Slupowski is a marketing expert in web 2.0, social networks, social TV, connected TV, wellness and personal data services and mobile multimedia services (apps, widget, etc.). He founded a start-up called Waptoo in 1999, one of the first companies to believe in the opportunities around mobile data, creating WAP and mobile services and proposing some automated mobile testing software, for companies and telcos, which has been acquired by Lagardere Group. Patrice is a graduate in Information Science and Technology from Paris-Dauphine University.
